Environment

Climate & GHG Emissions

Corelix is actively working to reduce its carbon footprint across all manufacturing and logistics operations. We have committed to a 40% reduction in direct greenhouse gas emissions by 2030 and net-zero operations by 2035.

Our emissions reduction programme includes energy efficiency improvements at our production facilities, transition to renewable energy sources, optimisation of logistics routes, and investment in lower-emission catalyst manufacturing technologies.

Our Pillars

Our Sustainability Commitments

01
Biodiversity

We assess and manage biodiversity risks at all operational sites, implementing measures to protect local ecosystems and minimise land use impact from our manufacturing activities.

02
Water Stewardship

We implement closed-loop water systems and strict wastewater management protocols at all production sites, aiming to reduce water consumption by 30% by 2028.

03
Acceptance & Belonging

We are building a diverse, equitable, and inclusive organisation. Our people strategy prioritises equal opportunity, cultural respect, and creating an environment where every team member can thrive.

04
Social Investment

Corelix invests in the communities where we operate through education programmes, local employment initiatives, and partnerships with development organisations across Africa, Europe, and Asia.

05
Innovation & Technology

We invest in next-generation catalyst R&D that helps our clients achieve cleaner, more energy-efficient operations — reducing emissions at scale through better chemistry.

06
Reporting & Transparency

We publish an annual Sustainability Report aligned with GRI and TCFD frameworks, providing full transparency on our environmental performance, social impact, and governance practices.
