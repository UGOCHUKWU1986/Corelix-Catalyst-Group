Our Company

Who We Are

About Corelix Catalyst Group

We are a globally focused catalyst manufacturing and export company dedicated to delivering advanced catalytic solutions that drive industrial performance and efficiency. Founded in 1974, we operate with a commitment to technical excellence, product integrity, and seamless international distribution.

We produce and supply high-quality industrial catalysts designed to improve efficiency and performance in critical processes. Our services include catalyst manufacturing, bulk supply, and international export to clients across multiple regions.

We work closely with industries such as oil and gas, petrochemicals, and industrial processing, ensuring our products meet strict performance and quality standards. From production to delivery, we are committed to providing reliable solutions that support operational excellence worldwide.

Our Journey

Our History

For over five decades, Corelix has been at the forefront of industrial catalyst manufacturing, growing from a regional supplier into a trusted name in international markets.

1974
Company Founded

Corelix Catalyst Group was established with a founding vision to produce premium-quality industrial catalysts for the refining and petrochemical sectors. Operations began from our first production facility, serving clients across Europe.

1982
International Expansion Begins

Following rapid growth in European markets, Corelix launched its first international export programme, establishing supply agreements with refineries across the Middle East and North Africa.

1995
R&D Centre Established

We opened our dedicated Research & Development Centre, investing in next-generation catalyst formulations. This marked a turning point — moving from manufacturing excellence to global innovation leadership.

2003
Asia-Pacific Operations Launch

Corelix entered the Asia-Pacific region, securing long-term supply contracts with major petrochemical and refining companies in Singapore, South Korea, and Japan.

2010
United Kingdom Headquarters Established

Our global headquarters relocated to the United Kingdom, positioning Corelix at the heart of international finance and industry — enabling stronger partnerships with multinational clients and investors worldwide.

2016
West Africa Market Entry

Corelix established strategic supply partnerships across West Africa, supporting the growing refining and petrochemical industry in Nigeria, Ghana, and Senegal.

2020
Sustainability Commitment

We launched our formal Sustainability Strategy, committing to carbon reduction targets, responsible manufacturing, and development of next-generation low-emission catalyst technologies.

Today
Global Leader in Catalyst Supply

Corelix today serves over 500 industrial clients in more than 40 countries across three continents, with a team of world-class engineers, chemists, and industry specialists committed to powering the industries of tomorrow.
