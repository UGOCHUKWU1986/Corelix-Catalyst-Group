Product Line 01

Refining Catalysts

Our refining catalysts are engineered for the demanding conditions of modern refineries, delivering improved fuel yields, reduced energy consumption, and extended catalyst life. We supply catalysts for all major refining processes including hydrotreating, hydrocracking, catalytic reforming, and fluid catalytic cracking (FCC).

  • Hydrotreating catalysts for sulphur removal and diesel quality improvement
  • Hydrocracking catalysts for maximum distillate and naphtha yield
  • Catalytic reforming catalysts for high-octane gasoline production
  • FCC catalysts for maximum conversion and light olefin production
  • Guard bed and demetallisation catalysts for feed pre-treatment

Product Line 02

Petrochemical Catalysts

Our petrochemical catalyst range is designed to maximise selectivity and yield across all major petrochemical production pathways. From ethylene and propylene to aromatics and speciality chemicals, our catalysts support the full spectrum of petrochemical manufacturing.

  • Steam cracking catalysts for ethylene and propylene production
  • Aromatics catalysts for benzene, toluene, and xylene production
  • Dehydrogenation catalysts for propylene and butadiene
  • Oxidation catalysts for organic acid and aldehyde production
  • Polymerisation catalysts for polymer-grade monomers

Product Line 03

Industrial Processing Catalysts

Beyond refining and petrochemicals, Corelix supplies specialised catalysts for a wide range of industrial chemical processes including ammonia synthesis, methanol production, hydrogen generation, and environmental applications.

  • Ammonia synthesis catalysts for fertiliser production
  • Methanol synthesis catalysts for fuel and chemical production
  • Steam methane reforming catalysts for hydrogen production
  • Environmental catalysts for emissions reduction and compliance
  • Shift conversion catalysts for syngas processing

Product Line 04

Adsorbents

Our range of high-performance adsorbents is designed for gas purification, liquid-phase treatment, and molecular separation applications. Available in various formats including pellets, granules, and powder forms.

  • Molecular sieves for gas drying and purification
  • Activated alumina for moisture and fluoride removal
  • Silica gel for desiccation and chromatography
  • Activated carbon for organic contaminant removal
  • Zeolite adsorbents for selective separation
