Reference

Glossary of Terms

A reference guide to key terminology used across the catalyst and industrial process industry.

Catalyst

A substance that increases the rate of a chemical reaction without being consumed in the process. Industrial catalysts are used to accelerate refining and chemical manufacturing processes.

Hydrocracking

A refining process that uses hydrogen and a catalyst to break down heavy hydrocarbon molecules into lighter, higher-value products such as diesel and jet fuel.

Hydrotreating

A catalytic process used to remove sulphur, nitrogen, and metals from petroleum fractions, improving fuel quality and reducing environmental emissions.

Adsorbent

A material that causes molecules to adhere to its surface, used in purification processes to remove unwanted substances from gases and liquids.
