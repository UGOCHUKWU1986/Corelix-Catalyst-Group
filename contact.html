Our Standards

Integrity Helpline

Corelix is committed to the highest standards of ethical business conduct. Our Integrity Helpline provides a confidential channel for employees, contractors, and third parties to report concerns about unethical behaviour, fraud, bribery, or violations of our Code of Conduct.

All reports are treated with the strictest confidence. Retaliation against anyone who raises a genuine concern in good faith is strictly prohibited.

integrity@corelixcatalysts.com

Suppliers

We work with a global network of suppliers and contractors who share our commitment to quality, ethics, and sustainability. If you are interested in becoming a Corelix-approved supplier, please contact our procurement team with full details of your products and services.

All suppliers are expected to comply with our Supplier Code of Conduct, which outlines our requirements on labour standards, environmental responsibility, and anti-corruption.
